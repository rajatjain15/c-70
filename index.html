What's New In Sree Gokulam Public school

Sree Gokulam Public School is located 3 kms away from Chengalpattu. It caters to students from Pre-KG to XII Std. The school was blossomed out of the great educational vision of Sri Gokulam Gopalan, the founder of Gokulam group who squeezed out his efforts on nurturing the developing society with education through young minds. Established in the year 2012, Sree Gokulam Public School has become the most preferred educational institution in Chengalpattu. The school is located in a picturesque place and the sprawling campus spreads over 5 acres. The enchanting infrastructure creates a signature of its own and a hall mark for the best learning experience of the students. We are here to bring out the star of perfection with a unique educational path way and our efforts bloom into service through a compassionate focus on the educational platform to develop in itself. We have an academic fraternity of qualified and creative teachers with good experience who make the students develop the men/ women in term rather than being moulded by other’s style. Being inspired by Sri Gokulam Gopalan, the school provides an opportunity for an individual’s overall development without a hiccup in a vitalizing environment for a better nation.
